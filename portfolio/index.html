To make this piece I used Adobe Photoshop and Audacity. I put the images into Photoshop and saved the files as audio so I was able to put them into Audacity. Afterwards, I used multiple audio effects on the audio and then exported them. I then got the image by using Photoshop to convert it back into an image. This was all about experimenting with glitched images and the different effects they can give. Some of the photos are my orignal ones while others are from different places online.
